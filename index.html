About Us

Cultivated for centuries, jute has sustained livelihoods and ecosystems alike. At Atashi, we bridge the gap between jute mills and companies seeking sustainable, high-quality jute products — fostering a circular economy that replaces plastic and empowers communities.

Our Value Proposition

  • For Jute Mills: Access to broader markets, streamlined operations, and demand insights.
  • For Buyers: Consistent supply, customized solutions, and verified sustainability benefits.

Revenue Streams

  • Commission on jute transactions and partnerships
  • Fees for quality assurance, customization, and market insights
  • Income from government subsidies and export facilitation
